ABOUT US

Etnobal Games is a video game studio created in 2021 by a video game enthusiast from the province of Quebec in Canada. The studio only has an online presence, we do not have a physical office.

For the moment, our expertise is focused on the Unity game engine and web game development in PHP. We also have a good knowledge of Board Game Arena Studio.

Our first game is scheduled for 2024.

Our first game is expected to be released in 2024. We are developing our expertise on multiple platforms. You can consult the list of our games in the table below.

THE STORY OF A VILLAGE

The story of a village is a game where the player has the pleasure to manage a medieval village and its citizens. The goal of the game is to make the village flourish while keeping in mind the happiness of its occupants. You have the possibility to build a medieval village in your image. Every citizen of your village is unique! He has a family, a home, a job and hobbies. This person also has skills and talents that he will develop over time.

For more information, see the official website.

MAINLAND

Mainland Online, is the online multiplayer version of the excellent Mainland game. In order to ensure the survival of your people in a chaotic new world, you need to place maps in order to create and expand territories. Your mainland is your largest territory. To accumulate as many points as possible at the end of the game (and therefore dominate the world) you need to maximize your Mainland and try to include value cards. Play your cards smart to keep your Mainland strong and united. In this sense, the goal of the game is also to block the territorial expansion of your opponents and prevent the development of their Mainland.

For more information, see the official website.
