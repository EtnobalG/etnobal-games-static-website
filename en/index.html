Etnobal Games is a video game studio created in 2021 by a video game fanatic from the province of Quebec in Canada. At the moment, our expertise is focused on the Unity game engine and web game development. The studio only has an online presence, we do not have a physical office. We are currently in the development of our first game with Unity. Information on this one has still not been revealed.
